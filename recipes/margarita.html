Margarita

One of Archer's many cocktails of choice: the classic margarita.

A seemingly simple drink until you try to make one without doing your research in front of your friends, the margarita can be taken to soaring highs or devastating lows with five simple ingredients and a cocktail shaker. Come hang out and let's celebrate together/apart!

Archer's Margarita Ingredients:

  • 45 ml freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 90 ml agave tequila
  • 45 ml Cointreau
  • Ice
  • Salt, for garnish (optional)

Margarita Method:

  1. If a salted rim is preferred, start by cutting a lime in half then rubbing the lime on the rim of the glass before dipping glass down into kosher salt.
  2. In a shaker, add 3 ounces of agave tequila, 1 ½ ounces of Cointreau, and 1 ½ ounces of freshly squeezed lime juice.
  3. Pour into a shaker and shake vigorously before pouring into the salt-rimmed glass full of ice.
  4. Garnish with a slice of lime on the side of the glass.
